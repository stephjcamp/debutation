Taylor Swift Albums

Taylor Swift had her debut in 2006, at 16 years old, with her self-titled album. Since then, she has consistenly released a new album every two years. She is the first and only artist in history to have won Album of the Year at the Grammys four times, for the albums Fearless, 1989, folklore, and Midnights. In 2023, she began what would soon become the highest grossing stadium tour of all time, called "The Eras Tour". A 3.5 hour musical spectacle, in this tour Taylor celebrates 17 years of music by singing a selection of hits and album-tracks from every single one of her records.
