Taylor Swift Albums

Taylor Swift had her debut in 2006, at 16 years old, with her self-titled album. Since then, she has consistenly released a new album every two years. She is the first and only artist in history to have won Album of the Year at the Grammys four times, for the albums Fearless, 1989, folklore, and midnights. See the full cronological list of her albums below.

  1. Taylor Swift (2006)
  2. Fearless (2008)
  3. Speak Now (2010)
  4. Red (2012)
  5. 1989 (2014)
  6. reputation (2017)
  7. Lover (2019)
  8. folklore (2020)
  9. evermore (2020)
  10. Midnights (2022)
  11. The Tortured Poets Department (2024)
